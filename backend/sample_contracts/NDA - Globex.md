# Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into between Globex Corporation and the Recipient. The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship.

## Confidentiality

The Recipient shall keep confidential all information disclosed by Globex. The Recipient shall not use the confidential information for any purpose other than the evaluation. The confidentiality obligations survive for five years after disclosure.

## Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York. Neither party may assign this agreement without consent.
