EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between Stark Industries and the Employee. The Employee accepts employment on the terms set out in this agreement.

POSITION AND DUTIES

The Employee shall serve as Senior Engineer reporting to the Chief Technology Officer. The Employee shall devote full working time to the company. The Employee shall perform duties reasonably assigned by the company.

COMPENSATION

The Employee shall receive an annual base salary payable in accordance with the company's payroll practices. The Employee is eligible for an annual performance bonus. The company shall reimburse reasonable business expenses.

CONFIDENTIALITY

The Employee shall keep confidential all proprietary and trade secret information of the company. This obligation continues after the termination of employment. The Employee shall return all confidential materials upon departure.

NON-COMPETE

During employment and for twelve months thereafter, the Employee shall not compete with the company. The Employee shall not solicit the company's employees or customers. These restrictions are limited to the territory in which the company operates.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product created by the Employee belongs to the company. The Employee assigns all rights in such work product to the company. The Employee shall assist the company in perfecting these rights.

TERMINATION

Either party may terminate the employment for convenience upon two weeks notice. The company may terminate immediately for cause. Upon termination, the Employee shall receive accrued but unpaid compensation.

INDEMNIFICATION

The company shall indemnify the Employee for claims arising from the good faith performance of duties. The Employee shall not be indemnified for willful misconduct.

GOVERNING LAW

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.
