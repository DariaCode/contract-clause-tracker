# Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement is entered into between Acme Corp ("Provider") and the Client.

## 1. Liability

The Provider shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of this agreement. The total liability of the Provider shall not exceed the fees paid in the preceding twelve months.

## 2. Term and Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days written notice. The Client may terminate immediately if the Provider materially breaches any obligation.

## 3. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to keep confidential all proprietary information disclosed during the term of this agreement. This obligation survives termination for a period of three years.

## 4. Non-Compete

The Provider agrees not to provide competing services to direct competitors of the Client within the same territory for two years following termination.

## 5. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware.
